What is Phishing?

Phishing is when hackers send FAKE emails pretending to be a real company to trick you! 🎣

They want to steal your password, money, or personal information!

🚩 Red Flags of Phishing Emails

Look for these WARNING SIGNS:

🔴 Urgent Requests

"Click NOW!" or "Your account will close in 5 minutes!"

Real companies don't rush you!

🔴 Asking for Passwords

"Please enter your password here"

Real companies NEVER ask for passwords by email!

🔴 Suspicious Links

Email says "from bank.com" but link is "bnak.com"

Check the link carefully!

🔴 Strange Sender

"support@randommail.com" instead of official email

Check who really sent it!

🔴 Poor Grammar

"Please to update your accound imediately"

Real companies have good writers!

🔴 Suspicious Attachments

Files from unknown senders

They might have viruses!

✅ How to Stay Safe

✅ Good Habits

✅ Check the sender email address carefully

✅ Hover over links before clicking

✅ Never give passwords by email

✅ When in doubt, ASK AN ADULT!

✅ If You See Phishing

✅ Don't click any links

✅ Don't download attachments

✅ Report it to the website

✅ Tell a trusted adult

🧠 Test Your Phishing Detection!

Question 1: Which is a RED FLAG for phishing?

The email is from your bank

Question 2: What should you NEVER do in an email?

Reply to spam
Check who sent it

Question 3: When should you click a link in an email?

Always - that's what emails are for
