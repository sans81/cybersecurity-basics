Why Passwords Matter

Your password is like the key to your house! 🗝️ A strong password keeps hackers out. A weak password? It's like leaving your door wide open!

💡 Make Passwords STRONGER with these tips:

❌ At least 8 characters long
❌ At least one UPPERCASE letter (A-Z)
❌ At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
❌ At least one number (0-9)
❌ At least one special character (!@#$%)
❌ NOT a real word or name

🔐 Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

2FA is like having TWO locks on your door instead of one! 🔐🔐

What is 2FA?

It's TWO ways to prove you're really YOU:

1️⃣ Something you KNOW (your password)
2️⃣ Something you HAVE (your phone)

How Does 2FA Work?

1. You type your password ✅

2. An app or text sends a SECRET CODE 📱

3. You type the code to get in ✅

Hackers need BOTH to break in!

Why Use 2FA?

Even if a hacker gets your password, they can't log in! They don't have your phone! 📵

It's like needing BOTH a key AND a secret code!

Where to Use 2FA

🏦 Your bank account
📧 Your email
🎮 Gaming accounts
📱 Social media
🤖 Everywhere important!

⚠️ Password DON'Ts (Weak Passwords)

❌ DON'T use:

❌ "123456" or "password"

❌ Your name or birthday

❌ Your pet's name

❌ "abc123"

❌ Patterns on keyboard

✅ DO use:

✅ Mix UPPERCASE, lowercase, numbers!

✅ Random letters and symbols

✅ At least 12 characters!

✅ Different for each account

✅ A password manager to remember them
