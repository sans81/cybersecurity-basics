🌍 Module 1: What is Cybersecurity?

🔐 What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is like a shield that protects your digital world! 🛡️

Think of it like locking your front door to keep strangers out of your house. Cybersecurity protects your computer, phone, tablets, and all the games, photos, and information you store online.

💡 Think of it this way:
Just like you wouldn't give your house keys to a stranger, you shouldn't share your passwords or personal information online!

🎯 Why Should YOU Care?

Everything we do online needs protection - your favorite games, your school projects, your photos, your parents' bank information, EVERYTHING!

Bad people (called "hackers") try to steal information from computers just like thieves try to steal things from houses.
