You know how in real life, your parents say DON'T talk to strangers? Like if someone you don't know comes up to you on the street?

🌐 THE INTERNET IS THE SAME WAY! But it's even TRICKIER because:

  • 😷 You can't see who they really are

  • 🤥 They might lie about who they are
  • 💰 Some try to get your information
  • 😟 They might try to hurt your feelings

✅ NEVER give personal info to strangers
✅ NEVER meet someone from the internet IRL
✅ ALWAYS tell a trusted adult!

🚩 RED FLAGS - WHEN TO SAY NO!

🚩 "What's Your Real Name?"

Strangers asking personal info = DANGER!

Say NO!

🚩 "Send Me a Photo"

Anyone asking for pictures = RED FLAG!

Say NO & tell an adult!

🚩 "Where Do You Live?"

Questions about location = DANGER!

Say NO!

🚩 "Let's Keep This Secret"

Secrets from parents = BIG RED FLAG!

Tell an adult IMMEDIATELY!

🚩 "You're So Mature"

Adults giving weird compliments = DANGER!

Block & tell an adult!

🚩 "Meet Me in Person"

Internet person wanting to meet = NO WAY!

Say NO & tell parents!

✅ STAY SAFE ONLINE!

1️⃣

Talk to Trusted Adults

Tell parents about who you chat with

2️⃣

Never Share Personal Info

No name, address, school, or phone!

3️⃣

No Pictures with Strangers

Keep your photos private & safe

4️⃣

Block & Report

If someone seems creepy, block them!

5️⃣

Tell Adults About Red Flags

Parents & teachers WANT to help!

6️⃣

Remember: You're AWESOME!

You don't owe strangers anything!
